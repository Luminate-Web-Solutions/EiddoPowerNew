Our Services

At Eiddo Power, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to address the diverse needs of our clients in renewable energy, engineering, and energy management. We are also proud to operate within the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model, fostering collaborations that drive large-scale renewable energy initiatives.

Engineering Services

Eiddo Power actively collaborates with government bodies and private entities through the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model to deliver large-scale renewable energy projects. Our role in the PPP framework includes:

  • Project Design and Development: Working with public and private stakeholders to conceptualize and implement solar and renewable energy projects.
  • Execution of Grid-Scale Projects: Developing and operating grid-scale solar power plants that meet the needs of government utilities and private customers.
  • Sustainability in Infrastructure: Ensuring that all projects under the PPP model align with sustainability goals and provide long-term benefits to communities and stakeholders.
  • Long-Term Power Supply Agreements: Providing affordable and fixed-price renewable energy under Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) to utilities and commercial entities.

The PPP model allows us to leverage collective expertise and resources, enabling the successful delivery of impactful renewable energy projects that align with national energy transition goals.
